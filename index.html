Power Lifters

Data is everywhere, even a Powerlifting meet can yield tons of data. For this homework, I chose to go the bonus route and pick my own data set from the openpowerlifting org. Due to the enormous amount of data available, I decided to only present the last two years of data in this project. My findings are presented throughout this Site.

Distribution of Age

The histogram shows the drastic drop in contestants as you leave the 20-30 age range.

Equipment Use

The bar graph represents total weight lifted compared to bodyweight and then catergorized by equipment used at the meets.

Equipment Use By Gender

The violin plot represents the equipment use by gender and weight.

Age VS Weight

The box plot presented shows the age ranges for the competitors over the last two years. The dots outside the boxes represent outliers for age ranges 10 - 60.
